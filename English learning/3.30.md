## 一、短语 & 单词

### 1. take credit for / take the credit for
- **Meaning**: 因某事获得功劳（常用于强调“抢功”或“承认功劳”）
- **Example**:  
  He tried to take credit for the team's success.

---

### 2. take the blame for
- **Meaning**: 为某事承担责任 / 背锅
- **Example**:  
  She took the blame for the mistake to protect her team.

---

### 3. spout off
- **Meaning**: 信口开河 / 夸夸其谈（通常带贬义）
- **Example**:  
  He kept spouting off about things he didn’t understand.

---

### 4. smoosh (≈ crush / squash)
- **Meaning**: 挤压 / 压扁（更口语、更随意）
- **Example**:  
  Be careful not to smoosh the cake in your bag.

---

### 5. take one's chance / take a chance
- **Meaning**: 碰运气 / 冒险一试  
（更常用：take a chance）
- **Example**:  
  I decided to take a chance and apply for the job.

---

### 6. act casual
- **Meaning**: 表现得若无其事 / 装作很自然
- **Example**:  
  Just act casual when you see her.

---

### 7. cargo container
- **Meaning**: 集装箱
- **Example**:  
  The goods were shipped in large cargo containers.

---

### 8. think something through
- **Meaning**: 深思熟虑（把事情从头到尾想清楚）
- **Example**:  
  You should think it through before making a decision.

---

## 二、语法点

### 1. 现在完成时（Present Perfect Tense）

- **结构**:  
  have / has + 过去分词

- **核心语感**：  
  👉 过去发生，但对现在仍然有影响 / 结果仍然有效

---

### 2. I've been told vs. Someone told me

#### I've been told
- **含义**：我“被告知过”（强调信息现在仍然有效）
- **语感**：信息对现在有意义 / 仍然成立
- **Example**:  
  I've been told this place is really good.  
  （现在仍然觉得这家店不错）

---

#### Someone told me
- **含义**：有人告诉过我（单纯过去发生）
- **语感**：只是陈述过去事件
- **Example**:  
  Someone told me about this place yesterday.

---

## 三、句型 & 表达

### 1. open and shut case

- **Meaning**: 板上钉钉的案子 / 毫无疑问的事情

- **相关表达**：
  - It’s an open-and-shut case.  
  - This is pretty clear-cut.  
  - There’s no doubt about it.

- **Example**:  
  With all the evidence, it’s an open-and-shut case.

---

### 2. set someone straight

- **Meaning**: 纠正某人 / 让某人回到正轨

- **相关表达**：
  - set things straight  
  - put someone right  
  - straighten someone out

- **Example**:  
  I had to set him straight about what really happened.

---

### 3. think something through（句型扩展）

- **Meaning**: 仔细考虑

- **相关表达**：
  - think carefully about  
  - weigh the pros and cons  
  - consider all aspects

- **Example**:  
  You need to think this through before committing.

---
